Welcome to Bethlehem Hotel

Bethlehem Hotel is located in Batu (Ziway), 167 km from the capital city of Ethiopia. Since opening, we've been dedicated to offering travelers a blend of **comfort, warmth, and true hospitality**.

Prime Lakeside Location

We are ideally situated near the Jetty Road, offering easy access to Lake Batu (Ziway) for boat trips, and close to other major attractions.

Warm Ethiopian Hospitality

From our rooms with beautiful garden views to our freshly prepared dishes, every detail is made with care. Our staff is always ready to serve you and make your stay unforgettable.

Delicious Local Cuisine

Our restaurant features a wide variety of fresh, local, and international dishes prepared by our talented chefs, focusing on local flavors and quality ingredients.
