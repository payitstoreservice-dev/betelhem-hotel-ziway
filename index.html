Welcome to Betelhem Hotel

Betelhem Hotel is located in Batu/Ziway, 167 km from the capital city of Ethiopia. Since opening, we've been dedicated to offering travelers a blend of **comfort, warmth, and true hospitality**.

Prime Lakeside Location

We are ideally situated near the Jetty Road, offering easy access to Lake Ziway for boat trips, and close to other major attractions.

Warm Ethiopian Hospitality

From our rooms with beautiful garden views to our freshly prepared dishes, every detail is made with care. Our staff is always ready to serve you and make your stay unforgettable.

Famous Fresh Tilapia

Explore the Beauty of Ziway

Ziway, located in the heart of the Great Rift Valley, offers nature, history, and culture.

Lakeside Relaxation

**Relax the serene Lake Dembel (Ziway), watch the sunset, and enjoy the refreshing breeze.** A perfect way to unwind.

Batu Town Exploration

**Take a walk through the town of Batu (Ziway) to see local life, try coffee at a traditional coffee house, and experience the local culture.**

Boat Trips to Islands

**Take a boat trip to one of Lake Ziway's five islands, like Debre Sina, for historical sites and amazing bird watching.**

Rift Valley Birding

**Lake Ziway is a birdwatcher's paradise! See pelicans, cormorants, and many other endemic and migratory species.**
